Privacy Policy

Concerning the Web Site:

IP addresses of visitors to the trusted-bank.com site are used to help diagnose problems with the server and to administer the Web site. IP addresses are also used to compile general demographic information. There will be no attempts to identify individual users through their IP addresses, unless the user had malicious intents when visiting the site (for instance if the user tried to crack the website).

Sometimes an outside ad company is allowed to display ads on our site. These ads may contain cookies. Cookies received with banner ads may be collected by the ad company, and we do not have access to this information. The site trusted-bank.com itself does not currently use cookies, but it may use (non-persistent) cookies in the future. Cookies may be used to make sure you don't see the same ad repeatedly as well as to deliver content specific to your interests.

Concerning Mailing List(s):

If you subscribe to any mailing list featured on trusted-bank.com, your email address will be used only to send you the mails posted to this list and not for any other purpose. Email addresses of mailing list subscribers are never given or sold to third parties. You can unsubscribe from the mailing list(s) at any time, just by following the instructions sent to you when you subscribed.

If your mail contains a bugreport, bugfix or code contribution, your name and (in some cases) your email-address (but you can refuse this) will be added into the source code, tutorial, history-file, etc.

Concerning External Links:

This site contains links to other sites. The people behind trusted-bank.com are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.
